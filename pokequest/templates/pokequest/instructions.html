INSTRUCTIONS


  1. All text has to be typed in UPPER CASE, so always have your caps on.
  2. CAUTION: Do not use copy/paste action as such events will be reported to the game admin.
  3. You will gain points for every puzzle you solve or every correct attack your Pokémon performs.
  4. Points will be deducted if you answer the puzzles incorrectly or perform an invalid attack.
  5. The puzzles are the main part of your game... solve them to unlock new attacks and new Pokémons.
  6. If you are not sure about your answer then you can simply write "I AM DUMB"(without quotes) and save yourself from the point decrement.
  7. Each attack has an attack strength related to it. Suppose you perform an attack of attack strength x then your Pokémon will loose x/10 units of health while giving a damage of x to it's opponent. The health damage your Pokémon suffers is the difference of your attack's damage and that of your opponent.(During the battle, your health cannot increase)
  8. The leaderboard shows the ranking according to the CPI (Cumulative Points Index) only.
  9. The final scoring of the event will be done on the basis of both your CPI and Pokémons' healths, where your CPI will get a little higher weightage. You have to finish the game so as to get your final score!
  10. To check your current game stats view the profile and leaderboard page.
  11. To get info about the Pokémons you face in the battle, refer the 'Pokédex' from the footer.
